About SmartPark

An AI-powered urban parking platform for the modern city

Project Overview

SmartPark is a production-grade parking management platform designed for city-scale deployment. It integrates IoT sensors (Arduino/IR), machine learning prediction engines, and a modern web interface to provide real-time parking availability, AI-powered recommendations, and seamless booking across multiple urban locations — from shopping malls and airports to hospitals and universities.
